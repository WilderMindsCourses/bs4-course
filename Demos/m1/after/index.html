What Rules Were Added to Remove Pluto

Rule How it affects Pluto
1. Should orbit around the Sun. Pluto also orbits (though it's a irregular orbit around the sun).
2. Should have sufficient mass to become regularly shaped (e.g. is round). Pluto is round...duh.
3. Should clear out it's own orbit (e.g. not have asteroids flying around it). This is the one that nullified Pluto because its moons can be small, but in that spirit, not sure that Jupiter or Saturn should be a planet. Hrmph...
